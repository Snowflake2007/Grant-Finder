How it works

Six agents. Global funding. One pipeline.

Every step handled by a dedicated IBM Llama AI agent — from profiling to a tailored application for VCs, grants, accelerators and more.

📋
Step 1 · Profile

Startup Profiling

Extracts sector, stage, location, team size, funding preference, and need from your plain-English description.

✅

Global Eligibility Check

Funding Discovery

Surfaces deadlines, funding amounts, funding type (equity/grant/credits), and required materials for every match.

📝

Scored Review

AI scores your application out of 100 and gives funder-specific feedback on strengths, gaps, and what to improve.

About GrantFinder

Built on IBM watsonx.ai

GrantFinder is a multi-agent AI system built on IBM watsonx.ai and Meta Llama foundation models. It was designed to solve a real problem: most startups — especially first-time founders — spend weeks researching funding options that don't fit them.

We built 6 specialised AI agents that work in sequence: one to understand your startup, one to check eligibility, one to surface funding details, one to draft the application, one to produce a preparation checklist, and one to score and review it — all in under a minute.

Every funding source in our database is verified from official websites. We do not include unverified, informal, or suspected-scam sources. All AI-generated output should be reviewed before submission.
